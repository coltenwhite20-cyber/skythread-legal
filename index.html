SkyThread Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At SkyThread, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, username, display name, and password (encrypted).

Profile Information: You may optionally provide your airline, home base, role, years of experience, bio, and profile photo.

Content: We store posts, comments, messages, likes, and follows you create.

Location Data: If you use the In Town feature, we temporarily store your location. This data expires after 24 hours.

Device Information: We collect device tokens for push notifications if you enable them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the SkyThread service
  • Connect you with other aviation professionals
  • Send notifications about activity on your account
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Ensure safety and security of our community
  • Respond to support requests

3. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help operate our app (hosting, analytics)
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other users based on your privacy settings

4. Your Privacy Controls

You control your privacy through Settings:

  • Allow/block messages from other users
  • Show/hide your home base
  • Show/hide your email address
  • Enable/disable push notifications

You can change your display name anytime and delete individual posts, comments, and messages.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data while your account is active.

When you DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile and all personal information
  • All posts you created (and their likes/comments)
  • All comments you made on any post
  • All messages and conversations
  • All follow relationships
  • All notifications and In Town drops

This deletion is PERMANENT and IRREVERSIBLE. We retain NO data after account deletion.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text.

7. Third-Party Services

SkyThread uses Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, storage, and analytics. We use Apple Push Notification Service for notifications on iOS devices.

8. Children's Privacy

SkyThread is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your data (contact support)
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

Email: privacy@skythread.app

Support: support@skythread.app

We respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.
